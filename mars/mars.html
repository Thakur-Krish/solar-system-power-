Mars

Mars, also known as the Red Planet, is the fourth planet from the Sun. It has the tallest volcano and the deepest canyon in the Solar System.

Mars has a thin atmosphere composed mostly of carbon dioxide. It is home to the largest volcano, Olympus Mons, and the deepest canyon, Valles Marineris.
