Mercury - The Swift Planet

Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun and the fastest orbiting planet in our Solar System.

About Mercury

Mercury is the smallest and closest planet to the Sun. It has a very thin atmosphere, meaning it experiences extreme temperatures. Due to its slow rotation, a single day on Mercury lasts 59 Earth days!
