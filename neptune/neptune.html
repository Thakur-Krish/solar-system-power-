Neptune - The Blue Giant

The eighth and farthest planet from the Sun, known for its deep blue color and extreme winds.

Neptune is the windiest planet in the Solar System. Its atmosphere is made of hydrogen, helium, and methane, which gives it a striking blue color.

  • 🌍 Diameter: 49,244 km
  • 🌡️ Temperature: -214°C
  • 🌀 Strongest winds in the Solar System
  • 🌊 It has 14 known moons
