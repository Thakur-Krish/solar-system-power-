Uranus - The Ice Giant

Uranus is the seventh planet from the Sun, known for its stunning blue color due to methane in its atmosphere.

Uranus is unique because it rotates on its side, making it different from all other planets. It has a faint ring system and 27 known moons.

  • 🌍 Diameter: 50,724 km
  • 🌡️ Temperature: -224°C
  • 🔄 Rotation Period: 17.24 hours
  • 🛰️ Moons: 27 known moons
